A review on "REWORK"

During my last vacation, I have read REWORK by Jason Fried and David Heinemeier Hansson (both founder of Basecamp). Here is what I think about this book.

tl;dr: Holy shit. THIS. THIS. And a thousand times THIS. I love the pragmatism and lets move attitude combined with a provoking writing style.
